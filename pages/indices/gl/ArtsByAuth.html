Articles Indexed by Author

Author names are alphabetized. Each article entry includes title with journal volume & page number. Links A-Z are provided, at the top and bottom, for quick jumps into the author name list. The creator of this new index has attempted to "standardize" author names (e.g., by providing first names where only initials appeared); corrections are welcome.

At present, this General Linguistics author index includes volumes 24 (1986 [1984]) through 43 (2005 [2003]). It is possible that earlier volumes may be indexed, in the future, but higher priority will be given to indexing later volumes as they are published and time & resources permit.
