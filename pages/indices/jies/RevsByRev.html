Journal of Indo-European Studies

Reviews Indexed by Reviewer

Reviewer names are alphabetized. Each review entry includes the [book] author/editor if named, the title, and the journal volume & page number, etc. Links A-Z are provided, at the top and bottom, for quick jumps into the reviewer name list. The creator of this new index has attempted to "standardize" personal names (e.g., by providing first names where only initials appeared); corrections are welcome.

N.B. Our Journal of Indo-European Studies online index includes volumes 1 (1973) through 38 (2010). When we began publishing our index, JIES maintained no index of its own; but now that JIES has begun publishing an index, we anticipate that volume 38 will be the last in our series.
