Winfred P. Lehmann

Publications: Reviews, 1970-79

  • 1970. Linguistica. By A. Rosetti. (Janua linguarum, series maior, 16.) The Hague: Mouton, 1965. Romance Philology 24:169-173.
  • 1970. Machine Translation. Ed. by A. D. Booth. Amsterdam: North-Holland; New York: J. Wiley, 1967. Computers and the Humanities 4:265-268.
  • 1970. Parametric Linguistics. By Louis G. Heller and James Macris. The Hague: Mouton, 1967. Word 26:139-141.

  • 1973. The London School of Linguistics: A Study of the Linguistic Theories of B. Malinowski and J. R. Firth. By D. Terence Langendoen. Cambridge: The M.I.T. Press, 1968. Romance Philology 25:421-427.
  • 1973. Studien zur Sprachwissenschaft und Kulturkunde: Gedenkschrift für Wilhelm Brandenstein. Ed. by Manfred Mayrhofer. (Innsbrucker Beiträge zur Kulturwissenschaft, 14.) Innsbruck: Institut für Vergleichende Sprachwissenschaft, 1968. Language 48:161-165.
  • 1973. Word Order in Sanskrit and Universal Grammar. By J. F. Staal. (Foundations of Language, suppl. ser., 5.) Dordrecht: Reidel, 1967. Romance Philology 26:405-407.

Ed. by Manfred Mayrhofer, Wolfgang Meid, Bernfried Schlerath, and Rüdiger Schmitt. (Innsbrucker Beiträge zur Sprachwissenschaft, 12.) Innsbruck: Institut für Sprachwissenschaft der Universität, 1974. Kratylos 19:34-41.
  • 1974. Studies in Formal Historical Linguistics. By Henry M. Hoenigswald. (Formal Linguistics, 3.) Dordrecht: Reidel, 1973. Foundations of Language 12:147-148.

  • 1975. Observations on Trubetzkoy's Contributions to Phonological Studies. Review of Principles of Phonology. By N. S. Trubetzkoy. Translated by Christiane A. M. Baltaxe. Berkeley & Los Angeles: University of California Press, 1969. Romance Philology 29:40-57.
  • 1976. Inside China. By Peter Worsley. Baltimore: Penguin Books, 1975. Social Science Quarterly 57:489.
  • 1976. Structural Aspects of Language Change. By James M. Anderson. (Longman Linguistics Library, 13.) London: Longman, 1973. Lingua 38:376-378.
  • 1977. A Concise Grammar of the Older Runic Inscriptions. By Elmer H. Antonsen. Tübingen: Niemeyer, 1975. Colloquia Germanica 4:356-358.

  • 1979. German in Contact with Other Languages. Ed. by Carol Molony, Helmut Zobl, and Wilfried Stölting. (Monographien Linguistik und Kommunikations-wissenschaft, 26.) Kronberg: Scriptor, 1977. American Anthropologist 81, 1:141-142.
  • 1979. The Order of Words in the Ancient Languages Compared with That of the Modern Languages. By Henri Weil. New edition with an introduction by Aldo Scaglioni. (Amsterdam Classics in Linguistics, 1800-1925, 14.) Amsterdam: Benjamins, 1978. Historiographia Linguistica 6:403-408.
