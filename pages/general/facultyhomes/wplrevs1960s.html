Winfred P. Lehmann

Publications: Reviews, 1960-69

  • 1960. Handbuch des Sanskrit. Vol. 1, Grammatik. Part 2, Formenlehre. By Albert Thumb. Ed. by Richard Hauschild. 3rd rev. ed. Heidelberg: Carl Winter, 1959. Journal of the American Oriental Society 80:152-153.
  • 1960. The Metre of Beowulf. By A. J. Bliss. Oxford: Basil Blackwell, 1958. Journal of English and Germanic Philology 59:137-142.
  • 1960. Om et norsk skriftlig grunnlag for Edda-diktningen eller deler av den. By Didrik Arup Seip. Maal og Minne 1957:81-207. Oslo: Bymålslagets, 1958. Journal of English and Germanic Philology 59:509-511.
  • 1962. Estructuralismo e historia: Miscelánea homenaje a André Martinet. Ed. by Diego Catalán [Menéndez-Pidal]. 2 vols. Tenerife, Canarias: Biblioteca Filológica, Universidad de La Laguna, 1957-1958. Romance Philology 15:439-442.
  • 1963. Relativsatz, Attribut und Apposition. By Hansjakob Seiler. 2 vols. Wiesbaden: Otto Harrassowitz, 1960. Language 39:281-286.
  • 1963. Studia Gratulatoria Dedicated to Alberta Willem de Groot in the Year of His Seventieth Birthday. (Lingua 11.) Amsterdam: North-Holland, 1962. Romance Philology 17:444-449.
  • 1963. A Tentative Typology of Romance Historical Grammars. By Yakov Malkiel. Lingua 9:321-416. Amsterdam: North-Holland, 1960. Language 39:286-290.
  • 1963. A Word-Index to the Texts of Steinmeyer Die kleineren althochdeutschen Sprachdenkmäler. By R-M. S. Heffner. Madison: University of Wisconsin Press, 1961. Monatshefte 55:343-344.
  • 1964. The Aspectual Function of the Rigvedic Present and Aorist. By J. Gonda. The Hague: Mouton, 1962. Journal of the American Oriental Society 84:457-459.

  • 1965. Sravnitel'naia grammatika germanskikh iazykov. 3 vols. Moscow: Academy of Sciences, 1962-63. Voprosy Iazykoznaniia 6:122-132. Translated from the English by M. M. Makovskii.
  • 1966. Mediaeval German Studies: Presented to Frederick Norman, Professor of German in the University of London, by His Students, Colleagues and Friends on the Occasion of His Retirement. London: University of London Institute of Germanic Studies, 1965. German Life and Letters 19:292-296.
  • 1966. An Outline of a General Theory of Structural Relations. By Alphonse Juilland. The Hague: Mouton, 1961. Romance Philology 20:218-221.
  • 1967. Diachronic and Synchronic Aspects of Language: Selected Articles. By Alf Sommerfelt. (Janua linguarum, series maior, 7.) The Hague: Mouton, 1962. Romance Philology 21:67-75.
  • 1967. A History of the German Language. By John T. Waterman. Seattle: University of Washington Press, 1966. Glossa 1:75-82.
  • 1967. The Oxford Dictionary of English Etymology. Ed. by C. T. Onions, et al. Oxford: Clarendon Press, 1966. [and] A Comprehensive Etymological Dictionary of the English Language. Vol. 1, A-K. By Ernest Klein. Amsterdam: Elsevier, 1966. College English 28:625-628.
  • 1967. Stand und Aufgabe der allgemeinen Sprachwissenschaft. By Ernst Otto. With supplements by Ernst Otto and Gerhard Haselbach. 2nd rev. ed. Berlin: Walter de Gruyter, 1965. Zeitschrift für Mundartforschung 34:75-77.
  • 1968. Estructuralismo e historia. Vol. 3, Miscelánea homenaje a André Martinet. Ed. by Diego Catalán [Menéndez-Pidal]. Tenerife, Canarias: Biblioteca Filologica, Universidad de La Laguna, 1962. Romance Philology 21:353-354.
  • 1968. Handbuch der litauischen Sprache. Vol. 1, Grammatik. By Alfred Senn. Heidelberg: Carl Winter, 1966. Language 44:639-642.
  • 1968. Helhed og Struktur: Udvalgte Sprogvidenskabelige Afhandlinger. Selected Linguistic Papers with Detailed English Summaries. By Paul Diderichsen. Copenhagen: G. E. C. Gads, 1966. Acta Linguistica Hafniensia 11:119-122.
  • 1968. Problèmes de linguistique générale. By Émile Benveniste. Paris: Gallimard, 1966. Language 44:91-96.
  • 1968. The System of Sonants and Ablaut in Kartvelian Languages: A Typology of Common Kartvelian Structure. [Title also in Georgian and Russian.] By Th. V. Gamkrelidze and G. I. Machavariani. Tbilisi: Izdatel'stvo 'Metsniyereba', 1965. Language 44:404-407.

  • 1969. The Linguistic Sciences and Language Teaching. By M. A. K. Halliday, Angus McIntosh, and Peter Strevens. Bloomington: Indiana University Press, 1964. Romance Philology 23:130-131.
